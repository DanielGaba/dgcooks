🍯 Semi-Rye Express Honey Bread

Ingredients:

  • Rye flour: 150g
  • Wheat flour: 200g
  • Water (warm): 250ml
  • Honey: 2 tbsp
  • Instant yeast: 1 tsp
  • Salt: 1 tsp
  • Mixed seeds (sunflower, pumpkin, flax): 3-4 tbsp

Instructions:

  • Step 1: Dissolve honey and yeast in warm water. Let sit for 5-10 minutes.
  • Step 2: In a large bowl, mix rye flour, wheat flour, and salt. Pour in the liquid mixture and stir until a sticky dough forms. Add most of the seeds.
  • Step 3: Cover the dough and let it rise in a warm place for 45-60 minutes.
  • Step 4: Shape into a loaf, sprinkle the remaining seeds on top, and bake at 200°C (390°F) for 35-40 minutes until done.
